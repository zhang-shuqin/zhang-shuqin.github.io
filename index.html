About me

I am a PhD student in mathamatics. I am doing my PhD in Hunan University under the supervision of Prof. Yingqing Xiao. Now I am visiting the analysis group at University of St. Andrews (2023-2024) under the supervision of Prof. Lars Olsen.

My research interests mainly relate to multifractal analysis and dynamical systems. I have worked on L^q spectra, Fourier dimensions, divergence sets and tube measures of in-homogeneous self-similar measures. I am currently interested in directed graph constructions and dimension theory of fractal sets and measures.
